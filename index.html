    About TIMYC

    Founded in 2021 in Choma, Zambia, TIMYC is a youth-led organization committed to empowering young people through arts, leadership, innovation, environmental action and social change.


    We stand against drug abuse, gender based violence, early marriages and unemployment while promoting talent, entrepreneurship and hope.

    Our Projects

    🎬 Movie Production

    Training young people in acting, directing, editing and storytelling.

    🌳 Tree Life Cycle

    Environmental campaigns and tree planting in schools and communities.

    🤝 Carelift Foundation

    A community impact project under TIMYC dedicated to supporting vulnerable children, youths and families through education support, empowerment and humanitarian outreach.

    🚫 Drug Abuse Awareness

    Helping youths reject harmful substances and choose success.

    ⚖ GBV Awareness

    Promoting safe communities and ending violence.

    💡 Youth Empowerment

    Leadership training, entrepreneurship and life skills.
