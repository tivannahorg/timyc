About Us

Tivannah Innovations Youth Organization is a youth-led movement dedicated to empowering young people through creativity, innovation, film production, leadership and community development. Since 2022, the organization has been actively raising awareness on drug abuse, gender-based violence, arts development and youth empowerment across Zambia.

What We Do

Film Production

We create movies, short films and visual stories that inspire society.

Awareness Campaigns

We educate communities on drug abuse, GBV and social issues.

Youth Empowerment

We build leadership, talent and innovation among young people.
